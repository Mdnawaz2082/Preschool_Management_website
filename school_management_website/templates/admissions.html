Admissions

Admission Requirements

  • Child must be between 2 to 5 years old.
  • Birth certificate for age verification.
  • Two passport-size photographs of the child.
  • Valid ID proof of parent or guardian.

Admission Timeline

Step 1: Application Submission

Fill out and submit the online form with necessary documents.

Step 2: Review Process

Admissions team reviews the application within 5 working days.

Step 3: Parent Interview

Parents and children are invited for a short interview and visit.

Step 4: Confirmation

Confirmation email and enrollment instructions sent upon selection.
